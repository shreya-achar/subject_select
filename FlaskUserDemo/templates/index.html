Option Selection Deadline

Current Y10 students must complete their 2023 subject selection by 3.15pm on 9th September.

Year 10 Option Choices

All students in year 10 will learn the compulsory one-year course of:

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Physical Education
  • Health
All students in year 10 learn the compulsory half year course of Social Science.

Options: Year 10 students also choose from a range of subjects in two OR three other learning areas, according to their personal interest.

  • Technology
  • The Arts
  • Languages
Languages are full year courses. If choosing a language student will select only one more course from a different learning area.
